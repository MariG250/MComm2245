About Me

 

My Name is Mariana Garcia

An artist who was born and raised in a desert known as New Mexico! The state itself is already known for its reputation for the arts! Growing up, I always loved to draw. When I was five, my older brother Esteban Garcia inspired me to develop and grow with my art. Ever since then, I have eventually developed my skills and now am pursuing this in college. My goal as an artist is to obtain a bachelor's and be in the industry of animation!
